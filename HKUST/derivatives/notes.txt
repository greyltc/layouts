illuminated areas
a: area = 10 mm^2
r: radius = -sqrt(-a + h w)/sqrt(4 - π) ~= 0.530959
h: height = 2.845 mm
w: width = 3.6 mm
governing equation: (a-r*r*(π-4))/h=w

geometric dark area
da: dark area = 16.038 mm^2
dh: dark height = 0.8 + h = 3.645 mm
dw: dark width = 0.8 + w = 4.4 mm
governing equation: da = dw*dh
